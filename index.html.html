About Us

Barbican Playgroup has been providing high-quality early years education and care for children aged 2-4 years since 1975. Our dedicated team of qualified staff create a safe, stimulating environment where children can explore, learn, and develop at their own pace.

We follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) curriculum, which focuses on the holistic development of children through play-based learning. Our activities are designed to nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child.

"The manager and staff provide an excellently planned environment and curriculum for the children. Staff are great role models, interacting clearly and enthusiastically with the children throughout the playgroup session. Children are confident, highly motivated and eager to join in the activities. The children communicate well and children who speak English as an additional language are fully supported to help them develop their communication skills."

"The staff know and nurture the children really well. They understand the children's development and interests fully and plan activities that continually excite the children and feed their natural curiosity. The children have the opportunity to explore the community around them, benefiting from the museums and exhibitions close by."

Enrolment Information

We welcome children from ages 2-4 years. To register your interest or arrange a visit, please contact us by phone or email. We recommend registering your child early as places are limited.

How to Find Us

We are located within the Barbican Estate in Andrewes House. The nearest tube stations are Barbican (Circle, Hammersmith & City, Metropolitan lines) and Moorgate (Northern, Circle, Hammersmith & City, Metropolitan lines).
